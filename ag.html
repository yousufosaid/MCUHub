    Agatha: Coven of Chaos

    Description: After a mysterious boy wakes her from the spell and asks her for help, Agatha Harkness forms a coven with the women of Westview to enter the Witches Road, a place where wishes can come true. This is where the boy can get what he wants, and so can Agatha. But to get your wish, you must pass a series of dangerous tests. A group of powerful witches known as the Salem Seven come to Westview to seek revenge against Agatha and follow her coven into the Witches Road.
    Cast: Kathryn Hahn, Joe Locke, Isabelle Fuhrman, Aubrey Plaza, Patti LuPone, Ali Ahn, Emma Caulfield, Debra Jo Rupp, Sasheer Zamata, Maria Dizzia, Trey Sartorius, David Lengel, David Anthony Payton, Brian Brightman
